    328. Odd Even Linked List

    Approach:¶

    Make two linked lists, one for odd and other for even 1. Head of odd linked list will be the head itself 2. Head of even linked list will be head.next 3. Keep a curr pointer to keep track if current node is odd or even and accordingly assign that to either odd or even linked list
